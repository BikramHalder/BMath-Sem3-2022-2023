Let $ l_1 := \{0\}\X\R_{>0} $ and $ l_2 := \{(x,y) \in \R_{>0}^2 : y=2x \} $.
Then, 
$$
f\Big|_{l_1} = 0 \ {\rm and} \ f\Big|_{l_2} = \frac{1-8x}{5}
$$
So,
\[
  \lim_{\stackrel{(x,y) \to (0,0)}{l_1}} f = 0 \not= \lim_{\stackrel{(x,y) \to (0,0)}{l_2}} f = \frac{1}{5}
\]
Hence, the limit doesn't exist at $ (0,0) $
